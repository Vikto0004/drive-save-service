Cookie Policy

This policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on the Drive Save Service website. By continuing to use our site, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy. Cookies help us provide a personalized, convenient, and efficient service for our users.

Cookies are an essential tool for modern websites, making navigation more comfortable and tailored to user preferences. By continuing to use the site, you confirm your acceptance of the cookies described here. You can adjust your browser settings to limit or disable the use of cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They save information about your actions and preferences for a certain period, so you don't have to re-enter them each time you return. Cookies can be set by the visited site (first-party) or by third-party services (e.g., advertising platforms).

These files typically contain data about the server that created them, their lifespan, and a unique identifier. Some cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the site, while others are used for user behavior analysis and marketing purposes. There are session cookies (deleted when the browser is closed) and persistent cookies (remain on the device until deleted).

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use various categories of cookies to improve the functionality of the site and your experience. They serve different purposes, from ensuring stability to personalizing content and analyzing traffic.

  • Necessary Cookies: Ensure the basic functioning of the site, assist with navigation, and provide access to secure sections.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how you use the site (e.g., most visited pages), helping to improve functionality and speed.
  • Functional Cookies: Save your preferences, such as language, region, and login data, for a more convenient experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to display personalized ads based on your interests.
  • Social Cookies: Enable sharing content on social networks and monitor interactions with it.
  • Analytical Cookies: Help collect information about site visits, traffic sources, and user behavior to optimize content and site structure.

3. How We Use Cookies

Our objectives in using cookies include:

  • Ensuring the proper functioning of the site and improving its performance.
  • Saving your preferences and login data.
  • Personalizing content and special offers.
  • Analyzing user interactions with the site for optimization.
  • Displaying targeted ads tailored to your interests.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can modify the settings to block them or receive notifications. Note that disabling cookies may affect the site's functionality.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We collaborate with trusted partners—advertising networks, analytics services, and social platforms—that may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

6. Updates to the Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to modify this cookie policy. Any updates will be published on this page along with the date of the last modification. We recommend checking this document periodically to stay informed about the current rules.

In case of major changes, we will notify you via the site or email if you have provided your contact details.
