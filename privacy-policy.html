Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us using the information provided below.

1. Data Collected

We collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: For example, your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you voluntarily provide through our forms.
  • Usage Data: Such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and duration of visits.
  • Cookies: To enhance your experience on our website (see our Cookie Policy).

2. Use of Data

The data collected is used for:

  • Providing and improving our services.
  • Personalizing the user experience.
  • Responding to your inquiries and requests.
  • Sending marketing messages, if you have given consent.
  • Analyzing website usage to implement future improvements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share information with:

4. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, data transmission over the internet and electronic storage cannot be guaranteed as completely secure.

5. Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • To request access to your personal data.
  • To request correction or deletion of your data.
  • To withdraw consent for data processing.
  • To file complaints with relevant data protection authorities.
